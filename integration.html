INTEGRATION BEE

Integration Bee, 2022

Inspired by the annual integration challenge conducted at MIT, the members of PaAC organized the event with the same name 'Integration Bee' but integrated with its own twists and quirks! First held in September 2022 the event witnessed enthusiastic participation of over 100+ students from various branches, with the event culminating with the top 3 teams as the winners of the nerdy tournament.

FORMAT OF THE TOURNAMENT

  • Preliminary Round
  • Speed Solving
  • Relay
  • Nightmare Integral Solving
  • Hoard the Integrals
